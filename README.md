# Debating Sphere By Darsh Agarwal

## How to Use the App

### Getting Started
1. Open the app in your web browser (deployed on Vercel).
2. You'll see the main interface with a setup panel.

### Setting Up a Debate
1. **Enter Debate Motion**: In the "Debate Motion" field, type your debate topic. For example: "This House believes that social media should be banned for children under 16."
2. **Choose Your Side**:
   - Select "Auto Assign Side" to let the AI randomly assign you Proposition or Opposition.
   - Or choose "Proposition" if you want to argue in favor of the motion.
   - Or choose "Opposition" if you want to argue against the motion.
3. **Set Speech Time Limit**: Choose how long you have to speak in each round (3, 5, 7, or 10 minutes).
4. **Select AI Skill Level**:
   - **Beginner (Learning)**: Friendly, explanatory tone with speech cues. Good for practice.
   - **Intermediate**: Balanced detail with targeted rebuttals.
   - **Advanced**: Sophisticated nuance and deeper weighing.
   - **Professional**: Tournament-ready precision and impact. Most challenging.
5. Click "Start Debate" to begin.

### During the Debate
- The AI will start with an opening speech, which will be spoken aloud.
- When it's your turn, click "Record Response" to start recording your voice.
- Speak your argument within the time limit.
- Click "Stop & Submit" to end recording and send your response.
- The AI will analyze your speech (transcribed to text) and respond with rebuttals, which will be spoken.
- Rounds progress through Opening, Rebuttal, Counterargument, and Closing.

### Features
- **Voice Recording**: Record your responses directly in the app.
- **AI Speech**: The AI speaks its responses using text-to-speech.
- **Real-time Feedback**: See your transcript and AI's points.
- **Session Persistence**: Debates are saved temporarily during the session.

### Tips
- Ensure your microphone is enabled for recording.
- Speak clearly for better transcription.
- Use the time limit wisely; the app will stop recording automatically if exceeded.
- Experiment with different skill levels to improve your debating skills.

### Troubleshooting
- If recording doesn't work, check browser permissions for microphone access.
- If AI speech doesn't play, ensure your browser supports text-to-speech.
- Refresh the page if issues persist.

Enjoy debating with the AI!
